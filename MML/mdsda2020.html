Sea Ice Snow Melt Onset data Nikita Platonov

General

Data

(Not included) NSIDC: method (Drobot and Anderson, 2001), up to 2017 data (Anderson et al., 2019).

AHRA: method (Drobot and Anderson, 2001), data: reproduction.

MDSDA: method (Belchansky et al., 2004), data: original software.

PMW (Early Melt): method (Markus et al., 2009), data (Markus and Miller, 2020).

tentative: Experimental improvement of MDSDA.

Processing

Average value for region. Missing values are for case when region is covered less than 80% of AHRA coverage.

Non-parametric segmentation with “mean” contrast function (Lavielle, 1999; Lavielle, 2005).
