4 Восток моря Лаптевых – встречи Summer Ocean areas Direct observations
Location data
Habitat use characteristics		 Polar bears are rare. They were sighted during Kara-Winter-2014 (Petrov et al., 2018) and “Master of the Arctic – 2021” expeditions. There are biotelemetry data during period April 1994 – August 2016 (Mauritzen et al., 2002). Northern boundary is limited by distribution of Great Siberian Polynya during 2009-2018 in March-May.

Rererences: Kitschinsky (1969); Karpovitch (1969); Беликов (2011); Успенский (1989); Petrov et al. (2018); Mauritzen et al. (2002).

Spelling: Успенский = Uspensky, Беликов = Belikov.
